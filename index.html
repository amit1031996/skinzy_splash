What we do


Skinzy is a project headed by Dr. Dhananjay Kalbande , Professor in Computer Engineering Department of Sardar Patel Institute of Technology,Andheri West, Mumbai,India based on Medical images and data processing for early stage detection of skin diseases. This is a live project in collaboration with Seth G.S. Medical College and KEM Hospital since 2015 under the expert guidance of Dr.Uday Khopkar, Professor and Head of SKIN and VD Department,KEM Hospital.






How we do it


A dedicated Research Team in SPIT involved in designing of various Artificial Intelligence techniques including image processing and machine learning for detection of skin diseases in collaboration with dermatologist.






What we do it for


Skinzy is meant primarily for research purposes and for the betterment of the society, by serving as a technological aid. The research is not intended to substitute any dermatologist. It is intended to serve as a tool for preliminary diagnosis where the expertise of dermatologists is not available. All we want to do is make the world a better place.
